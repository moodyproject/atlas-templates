Our Story

From the aromatic streets of Kashgar to the heart of Manhattan

Abdul Ahad Bakri is the proud owner and visionary behind Caravan Uyghur Cuisine, a beloved family-owned restaurant tucked away in the Financial District.

Inspired by the rich tapestry of Uyghur culture, he embarked on a mission to introduce the authentic taste of Kashgar to New York — establishing one of the first Uyghur restaurants in the bustling food scene of NYC.

Through his enduring dedication, Bakri has woven a remarkable story of tradition, passion, and the universal love for great food.

The Menu

Signature Dishes

Big Plate Chicken

A hearty Uyghur classic — tender chicken braised with peppers, potatoes, and hand-pulled noodles tossed in a rich, aromatic sauce.

Lamb Kebabs

Succulent cuts of lamb, seasoned with cumin and chili flakes, grilled over open flame until perfectly charred and smoky.

Uyghur Naan

Sour, malty, and impossibly crisp — our traditional naan is dense yet porous, designed to soak up every last drop of broth.

Polo

Fragrant Uyghur-style pilaf with tender lamb, julienned carrots, and raisins — a celebration of silk road flavors in every bite.
